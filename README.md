# CVision – AI Powered Resume Scanner

> A smart recruitment assistant for resume analysis and AI-driven candidate shortlisting

---

## Project Overview

CVision is an AI-powered resume screening platform designed to assist recruiters in efficiently shortlisting candidates.  
The system leverages artificial intelligence to analyze resumes and generate summaries, relevance scores, and insights, helping reduce manual effort and improve hiring efficiency.

---

## Problem Statement

Recruitment teams often receive a large number of resumes for each job posting, making manual screening time-consuming, inconsistent, and prone to bias.  
Traditional keyword-based filtering methods fail to provide meaningful insights, leading to delayed hiring decisions and missed talent opportunities.

---

## 👥 Target Users (Personas)

### Recruiters / HR Teams
- Need to evaluate a high volume of resumes quickly
- Want unbiased and consistent shortlisting
- Require clear insights to support hiring decisions

### Job Applicants
- Want fair evaluation of resumes
- Prefer transparent screening processes
- Expect quicker responses from recruiters

---

## Vision Statement

The vision of CVision is to simplify and enhance the recruitment process by leveraging AI to intelligently analyze resumes, provide actionable insights to recruiters, and create a fair and efficient hiring experience for candidates.

---

## Key Features & Goals (Planned)

- Resume upload and parsing
- AI-generated resume summaries
- Resume relevance scoring based on job descriptions
- Recruiter dashboard for candidate management
- Redirection of shortlisted candidates to an AI-driven interview platform

---

## Success Metrics

- Reduction in resume screening time for recruiters
- Accuracy and relevance of AI-generated resume scores
- Recruiter and applicant satisfaction
- System response time and reliability

---

## Assumptions & Constraints

- Resumes are primarily submitted in PDF or DOCX formats
- Initial AI models may have limitations in accuracy
- System is intended for small to medium-scale usage in early stages
- Deployment and API usage may be constrained by cost and infrastructure limitations

---

## Planned System Architecture

CVision will follow a client-server architecture consisting of:
- A frontend web application for recruiters and applicants
- A backend API handling business logic and AI processing
- A database for storing user data, resumes, and analysis results
- Containerized services using Docker for consistent deployment

A detailed architecture diagram will be added in upcoming phases.
